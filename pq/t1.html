ONE OF A KIND GLASS ART

In a twist on the popular wedding sand ceremony, also called a unity sand ceremony, Australian glass artist Donna Cains is delighted to present Everlasting Unity Art. Donna Cains uses glass beads to create beautiful sculpted glass art as an eye-catching memento of your wedding.

Instead of a jar of coloured sand reminding you of your union, enjoy a stunning bespoke glass art piece.

Your one-of-a-kind glass sculpture will be handmade in the Brisbane studio following your wedding or union ceremony.

The colours and emotions of your glass sculpture begin as handmade beads that you blend together in front of your witnesses as your first official act as a united couple.

No two Everlasting Unity Art pieces will ever be the same, guaranteed.

Capture your special moment and cherish your bespoke art piece.

The Cains Cottage glass art studio is located at The Gap in Brisbane, Australia.
